About us

We are here to help you understand and find ways to improve your wellness, creating a better work environment for you and others around you. In 2019/20 work-related stress, depression or anxiety accounted for 51% of all work-related ill health and 55% of all days lost due to work-related ill-health(HSE). We are here to change the stigma around mental health.
